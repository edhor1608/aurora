# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus discussions on technical substance.
- Accept feedback and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or disclosure of private information.
- Sustained disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content and contributions that violate this policy.
